I am a master's student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University (thesis track), advised by Prof. Casey Taylor, and a Machine Learning Research Scientist at MasterControl. My work focuses on dependable small language model systems, multi-agent orchestration, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), knowledge-graph–augmented retrieval, and verifiable structured generation in regulated settings such as genomics and pharmacogenomics.

Previously, I completed a dual-degree B.Tech. in Computer Science Engineering and Biosciences Engineering at IIIT Delhi (Dean’s List, top 1% in JEE), and worked as a Senior AI/ML Engineer at BlueStacks/now.gg on large-scale traffic forecasting systems. I have also spent time as an AI/ML Scientist Intern at SAP Labs and as a research mentee in the Google DeepMind CS Research Mentorship Program.

In my free time, I enjoy playing table tennis, watching movies and anime, and reading.
