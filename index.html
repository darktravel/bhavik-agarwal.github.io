Bhavik Agarwal

I'm a Machine Learning Research Scientist at MasterControl and recent M.S. graduate in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University (advised by Prof. Casey Overby Taylor). My research focuses on Small Language Models, Multi-Agent Systems, and structured generation for trustworthy AI in safety-critical workflows.

Prior to my master's, I was a research mentee at Google DeepMind and worked as an engineer at BlueStacks and SAP Labs. I received my B.Tech. in Computer Science from IIIT Delhi.

Research

I work on trustworthy AI for regulated industries (life sciences, healthcare), focusing on multi-agent systems, knowledge graphs, and structured generation with verifiable outputs. Current interests include GPU optimization for LLM training/inference at the kernel level.
