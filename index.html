I am a thesis-track M.S. student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Casey Taylor, and a Machine Learning Research Scientist at MasterControl. I work on dependable small language model systems, including multi-agent orchestration, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), knowledge-graph–augmented retrieval, and parse-safe structured generation (e.g., ThinkJSON) for regulated domains such as genomics, pharmacogenomics, and compliance. I also maintain open-source models at MasterControlAIML and @bhaviktheslider.

Previously, I completed a degree in Computer Science Engineering at IIIT Delhi (Dean’s List), worked as a Senior AI/ML Engineer at BlueStacks on large-scale traffic forecasting, and served as an AI/ML Scientist Intern at SAP Labs. I was also a research mentee in the Google DeepMind CS Research Mentorship Program. Outside work, I enjoy playing table tennis, watching movies and anime, and reading.
